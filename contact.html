Brett Tanonaka
Brett Tanonaka is a freelance filmmaker based in the San Francisco Bay Area. As an innovative storyteller, Brett uses a contemporary blend of creative story writing and marketing to portray the vision of people and businesses through videography and photography. Concentrating his education on the effects the media has on society, Brett has developed a unique understanding of advertising, and combines his research of sociologists, psychologists, and digital marketing engineers to properly craft his media towards the appropriate audience. As a writer, cinematographer, and editor Brett is available and is more than happy to hear about any collaboration opportunity, big or small. He is here to work for you.
